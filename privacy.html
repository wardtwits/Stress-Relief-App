Stress Relief Simulator Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Stress Relief Simulator is designed to work entirely offline. The app does not require an account, does not show ads, does not use analytics, and does not send gameplay data to any server.

Information We Collect

Stress Relief Simulator does not collect personal data. The app only stores local preferences on your device, such as selected modes, themes, or other app settings needed to remember your experience between sessions.

How the App Works

The app works fully offline. It does not require internet access for normal use and does not transmit tap activity, gameplay behavior, or device usage information to us or to any external service.

Information Sharing

Because Stress Relief Simulator does not collect personal data, no personal data is sold, shared, rented, or transferred to third parties.

Third-Party Services and SDKs

Stress Relief Simulator does not use third-party analytics, advertising tools, or third-party SDKs that collect data about you.

Children’s Privacy

Stress Relief Simulator is not specifically targeted to children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

If the app’s data practices change in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated to clearly describe those changes before or when they take effect.

Contact

If you have privacy questions about Stress Relief Simulator, contact: privacy@stressreliefsimulator.com
